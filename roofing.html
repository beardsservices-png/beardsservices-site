Why Metal Roofing?

Metal roofing lasts 40–70 years compared to 15–25 for asphalt shingles. In Arkansas, where hail, ice storms, and high winds are routine, metal holds up where shingles fail.

It's also more energy efficient, reflecting solar heat instead of absorbing it — which matters during Mountain Home summers.

  • 40–70 year lifespan
  • Wind and hail resistant
  • Energy efficient — reflects heat
  • Low maintenance once properly installed
  • Increases home resale value
