About Beard's Home Services

Brian Beard has been working on homes in the Mountain Home area since the early 2000s — concrete, roofing, remodels, and just about everything in between. He runs Beard's Home Services as a solo operation, which means when you call, Brian answers. When the job starts, Brian shows up. No crews, no subcontractors, no strangers walking through your house.

He's straight about what a job will cost, straight about whether he can do it, and straight about the timeline. If something comes up mid-job, you hear it from him directly — not from whoever answers the phone at a big company. That's just how he works.

Serving Mountain Home, Gassville, Bull Shoals, Cotter, Lakeview, Flippin, and the rest of Baxter County.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you require payment upfront?

No. You pay only after the work is completed to your satisfaction. That's been the policy since day one and it's not changing.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes. Beard's Home Services is licensed and insured. You're covered on every job.

Will Brian actually be the one doing the work?

Yes. Brian does the work himself on every job — no crews, no subcontractors. When you hire Beard's Home Services, you get Brian.

Do you handle small jobs, or only big projects?

Both. A single leaky faucet or a full bathroom gut-and-rebuild — no job is too small or too complex. If it's your home, call and ask.

What forms of payment do you accept?

Cash and check. Payment is due after the job is complete and you're satisfied.

How do I get a quote?

Call or text (870) 321-1072, email brianb@beardsservices.com, or use the contact form below. Free quotes, no obligation. Brian will come out, see the job, and give you a real number.

What areas do you serve?

Mountain Home, Gassville, Bull Shoals, Cotter, Lakeview, Flippin, Clarkridge, and the broader Baxter County / Twin Lakes area. Brian also travels for larger projects — just call and ask.
