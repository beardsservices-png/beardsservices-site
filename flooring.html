Subfloor Leveling — The Step Most People Skip

The most common reason flooring fails prematurely isn't the flooring itself — it's what's underneath. Soft spots, rot, and uneven subfloor cause LVP to separate, tile to crack, and laminate to buckle.

Brian is particularly experienced with subfloor leveling and repair. He addresses what's underneath before laying anything on top. That's the difference between flooring that lasts 10 years and flooring that lasts 30.

  • Soft spot and rot repair
  • Self-leveling compound for uneven surfaces
  • Full subfloor replacement when needed
  • Proper underlayment selection for each floor type
